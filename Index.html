About me!

I'm a small time artist trying to showcase my creativity and maybe make a little money off of it, to support me going to college. I'm also using this website as a bit of coding practice so sorry if things seem a bit wonky. I am a furry, and I use the name Fettuccine Alfredo to protect my privacy online. My main moniker/mascot is also named Fettuccine Alfredo, after the pasta dish. He's an eastern dragon, something I felt showcased my love of cat-like creatures and dragon-like creatures.

What I use

I use a mixture of paper and pencil as well as a Ugee series S (S1060) Drawing tablet with Krita.
